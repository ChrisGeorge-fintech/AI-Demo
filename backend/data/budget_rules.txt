DEMO ORGANIZATION — BUDGET RULES & CHART OF ACCOUNTS (FY 2026)

== BUDGET LINES ==

1. OFFICE SUPPLIES
   Budget Code: OPS-001
   Annual Budget: R 18,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 2,000
   Approval Required Above: R 1,000
   Eligible Expenses: Stationery, printer consumables, paper, pens, folders, packaging materials.
   Ineligible: Personal items, furniture, electronic equipment.

2. IT & SOFTWARE
   Budget Code: ITS-002
   Annual Budget: R 120,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 15,000
   Approval Required Above: R 5,000
   Eligible Expenses: Software licences, cloud hosting, SaaS subscriptions, antivirus, domain registration.
   Ineligible: Hardware (see IT Equipment), personal software.

3. IT EQUIPMENT
   Budget Code: ITE-003
   Annual Budget: R 80,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 20,000
   Approval Required Above: R 8,000
   Eligible Expenses: Laptops, desktops, monitors, networking hardware, printers, servers.
   Ineligible: Software (see IT & Software), consumables.

4. TRAVEL & TRANSPORT
   Budget Code: TRV-004
   Annual Budget: R 45,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 5,000
   Approval Required Above: R 2,000
   Eligible Expenses: Fuel reimbursements, public transport, vehicle hire, parking, tolls for business travel.
   Ineligible: Personal travel, fines, vehicle purchase.

5. TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT
   Budget Code: TRD-005
   Annual Budget: R 60,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 10,000
   Approval Required Above: R 3,000
   Eligible Expenses: Workshops, conferences, online courses, professional certifications, study materials.
   Ineligible: Membership fees (see Professional Memberships), social events.

6. STAFF WELFARE
   Budget Code: STW-006
   Annual Budget: R 25,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 3,000
   Approval Required Above: R 1,500
   Eligible Expenses: Team lunches, staff events, wellness activities, gifts for milestones.
   Ineligible: Individual personal expenses, alcohol above 20% of bill.

7. PROFESSIONAL SERVICES
   Budget Code: PRF-007
   Annual Budget: R 300,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 50,000
   Approval Required Above: R 20,000
   Eligible Expenses: External audit fees, legal consultation, consulting, tax advisory, valuations.
   Ineligible: Recurring operational services (see Facilities), internal headcount costs.

8. MARKETING & ADVERTISING
   Budget Code: MKT-008
   Annual Budget: R 90,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 15,000
   Approval Required Above: R 5,000
   Eligible Expenses: Digital ads, print media, branding, events sponsorship, website development.
   Ineligible: Staff costs, non-business promotional items.

9. RENT & LEASE
   Budget Code: RNT-009
   Annual Budget: R 560,000
   Per-transaction Limit: R 50,000
   Approval Required Above: R 45,000 (CFO sign-off)
   Eligible Expenses: Office/warehouse rent, equipment leases, vehicle leases.
   Ineligible: Purchase of property.

10. UTILITIES
    Budget Code: UTL-010
    Annual Budget: R 35,000
    Per-transaction Limit: R 5,000
    Approval Required Above: R 2,500
    Eligible Expenses: Electricity, water, internet, telephone landlines.
    Ineligible: Personal mobile phones, TV subscriptions.

11. FACILITIES & MAINTENANCE
    Budget Code: FAC-011
    Annual Budget: R 40,000
    Per-transaction Limit: R 8,000
    Approval Required Above: R 3,000
    Eligible Expenses: Cleaning services, repairs, pest control, security, waste disposal.
    Ineligible: Capital improvements (must be capitalised).

12. BANK CHARGES
    Budget Code: BNK-012
    Annual Budget: R 6,000
    Per-transaction Limit: No limit (bank-initiated)
    Approval Required Above: N/A (auto-approved)
    Eligible Expenses: Monthly bank fees, transaction charges, SWIFT fees.
    Ineligible: Interest on loans (separate budget line).

13. MISCELLANEOUS
    Budget Code: MSC-013
    Annual Budget: R 12,000
    Per-transaction Limit: R 1,000
    Approval Required Above: R 500
    Notes: Last-resort budget line. Finance manager must confirm no other budget line applies.

14. POSTAGE & COURIER
    Budget Code: PST-014
    Annual Budget: R 5,000
    Per-transaction Limit: R 500
    Approval Required Above: R 300
    Eligible Expenses: Stamps, courier services, registered mail for business correspondence.
    Ineligible: Personal parcels.

== GENERAL POLICIES ==
- All transactions require a valid invoice or receipt.
- Transactions above approval threshold require written pre-approval from the relevant budget holder.
- VAT-registered suppliers: only claim input VAT with a valid tax invoice.
- Split transactions to circumvent per-transaction limits are strictly prohibited.
- Budget overspends require CFO approval before the expense is incurred.
